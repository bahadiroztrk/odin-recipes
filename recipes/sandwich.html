Tuna Egg Sandwich

Description

This tuna salad with egg is quick and simple to make into a hearty sandwich with your favorite bread. This is my grandparents' recipe, and they always tell me that this is the correct way to make a sandwich!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Can Tuna (Drained)
  • Chopped Celery
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Whole Wheat Bread

Steps

  1. Combine eggs, tuna, celery, and mayonnaise in a medium bowl. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Divide tuna mixture evenly onto two slices of bread; top with remaining slices of bread.
  3. Enjoy your meal!
